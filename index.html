Are you missing a tool you need to finish that backyard project?

Use My Tools allows you to borrow the tools you need to finish your project. Use My Tools is a network of "friendly neighbors" that are willing to loan you their tools. Search for specific tools or see what tools are available to you in your area. All you have to do to find the tool you need and to get started building is to create an account and start looking!

Would you like to help out your neighbors and lend some tools?

Are you tired of loaning out your good tools and forgetting who you gave them to? Use My Tools allows you to keep up with who has what and how long they have added. You'll never again forget where that trimmer went. This service doesn't work without people like you that are willing to lend some of your tools out to those that need them.

Frequently Asked Questions
Q: How much does it cost to use a tool that someone has listed?

A: All tools listed on Use My Tools are available to borrow from their generous owners free of charge!

Q: What do I do if I want to purchase a tool that I have borrowed?

A: We do no currently support any users with sales transactions. These transactions are often arranged between members on their own, but we don't help to facilitate any sales at this time.

Q: Can I borrow more than one tool at a time from one person?

A: Loaning multiple tools out to a single person is up to the discretion of the loaner.

Q: Loaning out a tool to a stranger seems like a silly thing to do.

A: That's not a question.


Our service relies on generous people that are willing to share their tools and grateful people that take care of things that don't belong to them.
